## thehomerestaurant-lounge
Frontend website for The Home Restaurant &amp; Lounge — Calabar. Single-file HTML/CSS/JS. Features scroll animations, embedded brand assets, WhatsApp CTA, and Google Maps integration.

## nav
Fixed navigation bar with logo, links, and reserve button

## hero
Full-screen split layout with headline and building photo

## .marquee-bar
Animated scrolling strip of brand highlights

## about
Restaurant story with banner image and perks grid

## menu
Numbered editorial menu list with categories and prices

## experience & reviews
- Four feature cards — AYCE, Happy Hour, Fireplace, 24hrs
- 4.5★ rating display with three guest review cards

## find-us
Split layout with contact info and embedded Google Map

## footer & .wa-float
- Logo, navigation links, and copyright
- Fixed floating WhatsApp button

## images/ logo.png
Crown shield logo — black background, gold design

## banner.webp
Street-facing signage banner with HOMEFOOD branding

## Design System
- Token
Primary background      #080808 (near black)
Gold accent      #f0d060
Dark gold      #c9a82c
Off-white text      #f8f6ef
Muted text      #888888
Display font      Cinzel (serif)
Body font      Lato (sans-serif)
